## Service eligibility

CoutureCast is currently intended for users who are at least 13 years old. The
main CoutureCast service is not available to children under 13.

Users aged 13 through 15 may create an account only when CoutureCast can place
the account into a pending guardian-consent state. Those users may not access
wardrobe, recommendation, community, or other account features that require
guardian approval until a guardian grants consent.

Users aged 16 and older may create and use an active account without guardian
consent, subject to the rest of these Terms.

## COPPA and guardian consent

CoutureCast collects birthdate during signup to determine whether the user is
eligible to use the main service and whether guardian consent is required.

If a user is under 13, they may not use the main CoutureCast service. Any future
CoutureCast Jr. experience for children under 13 will be governed by separate
terms, privacy notices, and verifiable parental consent requirements.

If a user is 13 through 15, CoutureCast requires guardian consent before enabling
wardrobe access. The guardian invitation process asks the guardian to review the
teen account request and confirm the level of access they approve. CoutureCast
records consent details, including the teen account, guardian account, consent
level, consent timestamp, and technical evidence needed for compliance and
security.

Guardians may revoke consent. When consent is revoked, CoutureCast may restrict
or disable the teen account's access until another active guardian consent exists
or the user becomes eligible for independent access under CoutureCast policy.

Guardians may request review or deletion of covered minor information through the
privacy contact listed in the Privacy Policy. Some records may be retained when
needed for security, legal compliance, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or
audit evidence.

## Account accuracy

Users and guardians must provide accurate age and consent information.
CoutureCast may suspend, restrict, or delete an account if birthdate, guardian,
or consent information is missing, false, expired, unverifiable, or revoked.

## Privacy policy relationship

The Privacy Policy must explain what information CoutureCast collects from teens
and guardians, how the information is used, how guardians may revoke consent, how
deletion or review requests work, and how long compliance records are retained.
